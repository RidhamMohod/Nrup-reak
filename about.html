COMPANY OVERVIEW

NRUP RECK

Nrup Reck was founded in 1999 by Mr Rajesh Shah having long standing experience in the trading of solvents and chemicals.

Nrup Reck has diversified into solvent recycling and recovery of various spent/ pre-used chemical solvents. We pioneer in the supply and trade of recovered solvents. Our Mission

Trading of Recycled/Recovered solvents by satisfying the need of our customers while realizing the value of waste solvents.

Through our solvent recycling and recovery expertise we create a clean product that can be reused by you, saving on the cost of virgin materials while maintaining sustainability and helping the environment.

Our aim is to promote the safe and economic management of post use solvents. Used solvents are potential hazardous waste and should therefore be fully recovered and reintroduced into reuse and only under exceptional cases be disposed of in appropriate manner.
