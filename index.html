ABOUT US

NRUP RECK

Nrup Reck was founded in 1999 by Mr Rajesh Shah having long standing experience in the trading of solvents and chemicals.

Nrup Reck has diversified into solvent recycling and recovery of various spent/ pre-used chemical solvents. We pioneer in the supply and trade of recovered solvents. Nrup Reck has established itself as a brand in recovered solvent distribution as well as trading industry. The focus of Nrup Reck since its intial stages from 1999 has been to become a leader in Domestic market with number of associates. The journey of our company has proved to been known for our distribution and recovery solutions.
